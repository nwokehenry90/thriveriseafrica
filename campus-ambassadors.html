Become a Campus Ambassador

Are you passionate about education, youth empowerment, and equal access to opportunities?

Join the ThriveRise Campus Ambassadors Program and become a voice for change in your university. As an ambassador, you'll represent ThriveRise Africa on your campus, inspire your peers, and help us spread our mission of driving access to education and skills development for young people.

WHAT THE PROGRAM ENTAILS

As a ThriveRise Campus Ambassador, you will:

Advocate for quality education (SDG 4) and decent work (SDG 8) among your peers.

Help organize and promote ThriveRise events, workshops, and webinars on your campus.

Serve as a link between students and ThriveRise, sharing resources like our Library, Tech Skills Initiative, and Academy.

Build a community of students passionate about social impact, innovation, and leadership.

Support outreach and awareness campaigns both online and offline.

WHAT YOU'LL DO

Represent ThriveRise Africa in your school.

Spread awareness about our programs and opportunities.

Connect with fellow student leaders and drive collaborations.

Share feedback, ideas, and stories from your campus community.

Take part in monthly challenges and campaigns.

What You'll Gain

Leadership Experience ? Develop real-world leadership, communication, and organizational skills.

Mentorship & Training ? Access mentorship sessions, workshops, and learning resources.

Networking Opportunities ? Connect with ambassadors from other campuses and professionals in education, tech, and social impact.

Career Growth ? Boost your CV with recognized experience in leadership and community building.

Certificates & Recognition ? Earn a certificate of service and exclusive opportunities to represent ThriveRise Africa in bigger projects.

Ready to Lead Change?

If you're ready to inspire and empower your campus community,
we'd love to have you join us!

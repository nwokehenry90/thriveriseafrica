Skills for Decent Work and Entrepreneurship

Equipping African youth with practical skills to thrive in today's economy.

Partner With Us

In today's world, a degree is not enough. Young people need practical skills to compete, secure employment, or start businesses. Thrive Skills is our empowerment track designed to equip youth with the skills they need for decent work, self-employment, and entrepreneurship.

Why It Matters

Youth unemployment is one of the biggest challenges in Africa. Millions of young people graduate every year without the practical skills required to enter the workforce. Thrive Skills bridges the gap between education and employment.

What We Do

The Thrive Skills program provides:

01

Vocational and Digital Training in high-demand fields.

02

Entrepreneurship Support, mentorship, and startup guidance.

03

Job Readiness Programs including CV reviews, LinkedIn optimization, and interview preparation.

04

Partnerships with Organizations and Businesses to connect youth with real opportunities.

Impact

By empowering young people with market-ready skills, we are:

01

Reducing youth unemployment.

02

Creating opportunities for self-sustaining businesses.

03

Building stronger communities and economies.

Get Involved

We invite businesses, mentors, and organizations to collaborate with us in training, hiring, and supporting youth.
